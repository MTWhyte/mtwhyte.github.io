I have been involved in teaching at the University of St Andrews since I
started my PhD.

# 2023-2024 Semester 1
This semester, I am teaching two tutorial groups and four examples classes for
MT2501 Linear Mathematics, a subhonours course in linear algebra.  

# 2022-2023 Semester 2
I was a demonstrator for MT3510 Introduction to Mathematical
Computing, an honours-level Python-based computing class. I taught in three
sessions per week for the four 'core' weeks, and six sessions a week for the
two 'pure' weeks.  

# 2022-2023 Semester 1
I taught two tutorial groups and two examples classes on MT2501 Linear
Mathematics.

I was a demonstrator for the MT2000 computing workshop, an introductory course
to Python, for twelve sessions over the first four weeks of the semester.
